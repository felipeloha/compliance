# INQ - Investigation Requests

## INQ-01 Legal assessment of investigative requests
Investigative requests from government agencies are subject to a legal assessment by qualified personnel from the cloud provider.

The assessment determines whether the government agency is relying on an applicable and valid legal basis and what further steps need to be taken.

## INQ-02 Informing cloud customers about investigation requests
The cloud provider shall inform the affected cloud customer(s) immediately upon receipt of an investigation request from a government agency, unless the applicable legal basis on which the government agency relies prohibits this or there are clear indications of illegal activities in connection with the use of the cloud service.

## INQ-03 Conditions for accessing or disclosing data in response to investigation requests
Access to or disclosure of cloud customers' data in response to government inquiries will only occur if the cloud provider's legal assessment shows that there is an applicable and valid legal basis and that the inquiry must be granted on this basis.

## INQ-04 Limiting access to or disclosure of data in response to investigative requests
The cloud provider's procedures for establishing access to or disclosing cloud customer data in response to government inquiries ensure that government agencies only have access to or insight into the data that is the subject of the inquiry.

If a clear definition of the data is not possible, the cloud provider anonymizes or pseudonymizes the data so that government agencies can only assign it to cloud customers that are the subject of the inquiry.
