# SP - Security Policies

## SP-01 Documentation, communication and provision of guidelines and instructions
Policies and instructions derived from the information security policy are documented according to a uniform structure. They are communicated and made available to all internal and external employees of the cloud provider in a relevant and appropriate manner.

The policies and instructions are versioned and approved by the cloud provider's senior management or authorized personnel.

The policies and instructions describe at least the following aspects:

- Objectives
- Areas of application
- Roles and responsibilities, including requirements for personnel qualifications and the establishment of deputization arrangements
- Roles and dependencies on other organizations (especially cloud customers and subcontractors)
- Measures for implementing the security strategy
- Applicable legal and regulatory requirements

## SP-02 Review and approval of policies and instructions
The cloud provider's information security policies and instructions are reviewed for adequacy at least annually by competent personnel.

The review shall consider at least the following aspects:

- Organizational and technical changes in the procedures for providing the cloud service
- Legal and regulatory changes in the cloud provider's environment

Revised policies and instructions are approved before they become effective.

## SP-03 Deviations from existing guidelines and instructions
Exceptions to information security policies and instructions undergo the risk management process as defined in OIS-06, including approval of the exceptions and acceptance of the associated risks by the risk owners.

The approval of exceptions is documented, time-limited, and reviewed for appropriateness by the risk owners at least annually.
