# AM - Asset Management

## AM-01 Inventory of assets
The cloud provider has established procedures for inventorying assets.

The inventory is performed automatically and/or by individuals or groups responsible for the assets to ensure complete, accurate, valid, and consistent records throughout the asset lifecycle.

The information collected about the assets is the information required to apply the risk management process (see OIS-07), including the measures to address these risks throughout the asset lifecycle. Changes to this information are logged.

## AM-02 Policy for the acceptable use and safe handling of assets
Policies and instructions for the permissible use and safe handling of assets are documented, communicated, and provided in accordance with SP-01 and address the following aspects of the asset lifecycle, as applicable to the asset:

- Approval procedures for acquisition, commissioning, maintenance, decommissioning, and disposal by authorized personnel or system components
- Inventory
- Classification and labeling based on the information protection requirements and measures for the determined protection level
- Secure configuration of mechanisms for error handling, logging, encryption, authentication, and authorization
- Requirements for software and image versions and the application of patches
- Handling software for which support and security updates are no longer available
- Restriction of software installations or use of services
- Protection against malware
- Remote deactivation, deletion, or blocking
- Physical transfer and transport
- Handling malfunctions and vulnerabilities
- Complete and irrevocable deletion of data upon decommissioning

## AM-03 Commissioning of hardware
The cloud provider has an approval process for the deployment of hardware used to provide the cloud service in the production environment, in which the risks arising from deployment are identified, analyzed, and mitigated. Approval is granted after verification of the secure configuration of the error handling, logging, encryption, authentication, and authorization mechanisms according to the intended use and based on the applicable policies.

## AM-04 Decommissioning of hardware
Decommissioning hardware used by the cloud provider in the production environment to operate system components requires approval based on applicable policies.

Decommissioning involves the complete and irrevocable deletion of data or the proper destruction of the data storage devices.

## AM-05 Commitment to permissible use and safe handling of issued assets and return
Internal and external employees of the cloud provider are demonstrably committed to the policies and instructions for the permissible use and safe handling of assets before they are permitted to use them, provided that the cloud provider has determined in a risk assessment that these assets could compromise the information security of the cloud service in the event of loss or unauthorized access.

Provided assets are demonstrably returned upon termination of employment.

## AM-06 Classification and labeling of assets
Assets are classified and, where possible, labeled. The classification and labeling of an asset correspond to the protection requirements of the information it processes, stores, or transmits.

The protection requirements are determined by the cloud provider's asset managers or groups according to a uniform schema. This schema provides protection levels for the protection objectives of confidentiality, integrity, availability, and authenticity.
