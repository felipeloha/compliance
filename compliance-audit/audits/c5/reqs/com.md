# COM - Compliance

## COM-01 Identification of applicable legal, regulatory, self-imposed or contractual requirements
The legal, regulatory, self-imposed and contractual requirements relevant to the information security of the cloud service, as well as the cloud provider's procedures for complying with these requirements, are explicitly defined and documented.

## COM-02 Guidelines for planning and conducting audits
Policies and instructions with specifications for planning and conducting audits are documented, communicated, and provided in accordance with SP-01 and address the following aspects:

- Restriction of read-only access to system components in accordance with the agreed audit schedule and as necessary to perform the activities
- Activities that could lead to disruptions to the cloud service or violations of contractual requirements are performed during scheduled maintenance hours or outside of peak load times
- Logging and monitoring of activities

## COM-03 Internal audits of the information security management system
Qualified personnel conduct regular internal audits, at least annually, to verify compliance of the information security management system with relevant and applicable legal, regulatory, self-imposed, or contractual requirements (see COM-01), as well as adherence to policies and work instructions (see SP-01) within its scope (see OIS-01).

Identified vulnerabilities and deviations are subject to risk assessment in accordance with the risk management process (see OIS-06), and mitigation measures are defined and tracked (see OPS-18).

## COM-04 Information on the information security performance and management review of the ISMS
The cloud provider's top management is regularly informed about the information security performance within the scope of the ISMS to ensure its continued suitability, adequacy, and effectiveness.

This information is incorporated into the management review of the ISMS at least annually.
